Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-02

Welcome to HyperGameZ — a free online HTML5 gaming platform. Your privacy matters to us, and we’re committed to keeping your personal information secure and handling it responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and games.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Details such as your name and email address that you may voluntarily provide through contact or feedback forms. These are used only to respond to your inquiries.
  • Local Device Storage: Our games use your browser’s localStorage to save your best scores and preferences. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.
  • Basic Analytics: We collect aggregated, anonymous data such as total visits and popular pages. This helps us improve performance and enhance user experience. We do not collect precise locations or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the games and website.
  • To respond to your messages and offer customer support.
  • To analyze usage trends and enhance gameplay balance and features.

3. Local Storage & High Scores

Your game scores are stored locally on your browser using localStorage. You can clear this information anytime by selecting the “Clear Local Scores” option within the Games hub or by deleting your browser’s site data.

4. Cookies

We use only minimal cookies that are necessary for essential functionality and anonymous analytics. For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data in transit and at rest. While no system is completely secure, we encourage users to use strong, unique passwords when contacting us or creating profiles.

6. Children’s Privacy

HyperGameZ is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately for removal.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at:
Email: support@hypergamez.com
