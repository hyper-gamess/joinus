Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-02

At HyperGameZ, we use cookies and similar technologies to ensure our website and games run smoothly and provide a better user experience. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device, remember preferences, and enhance overall usability. Cookies do not contain personal data unless you choose to share that information.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These are required for the core functionality of our website and games, such as saving basic settings or remembering your interface preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: We collect anonymous, aggregated statistics — such as page visits and engagement time — to help us understand how users interact with the platform and improve performance.

3. Local Storage in Games

Apart from cookies, our games use your browser’s localStorage to save your best scores and in-game preferences. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear this information anytime from the Games hub (“Clear Local Scores”) or through your browser settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies directly through your browser’s privacy settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies altogether or receive alerts when a site attempts to store cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may limit some website features or affect gameplay performance.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Cookie Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted here with an updated “Last Updated” date at the top of the page.
